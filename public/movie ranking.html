The Best Movies According To Ranita

My top 3 movies

Dead Poets Society

One of the best movies I've ever watched—beautifully raw and inspiring. It had me in tears.

Grave of the Fireflies

Cannot explain how devastated it made me feel. But that heartbreak is what makes it so unforgettable to me.

Your Name Engraved Herein

My gay awakening. The bittersweet yet realistic ending makes the movie feel so deeply beautiful to me.
